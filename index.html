About

"Block Blast" is a free and popular block puzzle game that is your best choice when you want to pass the casual time and challenge your brain. The goal of this block puzzle game is easy yet fun: match and clear as many colored blocks as possible on the board.

The block puzzle game features two fun and addictive modes: Classic Block Puzzle and Block Adventure Mode, offering a cozy and satisfying gaming experience. Additionally, "Block Blast" is completely free, requiring no WiFi or internet connection, allowing you to take on the challenge of logic puzzles even in offline mode.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Block Blast!

How do I play Block Blast?

To play Block Blast, simply drag and place the blocks onto the game board. Try to form complete rows or columns to clear them and make space for new blocks. The goal is to keep the board from filling up while scoring as many points as possible.

Is Block Blast free to play?

Yes, Block Blast is completely free to download and play. There are optional in-app purchases available, but they are not required to enjoy the full game experience.

Do I need an internet connection to play?

No, Block Blast can be played offline. Once you've downloaded the game, you can enjoy it anytime, anywhere, without needing an internet connection.

How do I earn more points?

To earn more points in Block Blast, try to clear multiple rows or columns at once. Clearing multiple lines simultaneously will award you bonus points. Also, try to use all the blocks efficiently to maximize your score.

How do I save my progress?

Your progress in Block Blast is automatically saved as you play. When you exit the game and return later, you can continue from where you left off. Make sure not to uninstall the game if you want to keep your progress.

What are the different game modes?

Block Blast features two main game modes: Classic Block Puzzle and Block Adventure Mode. In Classic mode, you play endlessly until the board fills up. In Adventure mode, you progress through various levels with different challenges and objectives.
